About me

I'm Menno Jak — a software developer with a strong focus on Artificial Intelligence. I'm driven to create technology that makes a meaningful, positive impact.

I recently completed my HBO-ICT degree at the Christelijke Hogeschool Ede (CHE), specializing in software development with a minor in AI. During my studies, I worked on real-world projects at organizations like the Dutch Food and Consumer Product Safety Authority (NVWA), Brabantse Delta Water Authority, Albert Schweitzer Hospital, and BIM4Production — gaining experience in AI-driven development and practical software engineering.

I’m currently pursuing a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence at Utrecht University, where I’m deepening my understanding of AI theory and its applications.

Whether coding, researching, or collaborating, I aim to build thoughtful solutions that combine technical depth with clear, purposeful design.
